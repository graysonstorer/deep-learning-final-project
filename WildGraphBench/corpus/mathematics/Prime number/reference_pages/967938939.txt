Geometry of quantum states : an introduction to quantum entanglement
====================================================================

Authors: [Ingemar Bengtsson](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=au=%22Bengtsson%2C%20Ingemar%22) (Author), [Karol Życzkowski](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=au=%22Z%CC%87yczkowski%2C%20Karol%22) (Author)

Summary: The second edition of a book that has become a standard reference for researchers working on geometric aspects of quantum entanglement and quantum theory of information. Its comprehensive style makes it suitable for students of physics, information science, and mathematics wishing to learn more about quantum mechanics and quantum entanglement.

Print Book, English, 2017

Edition: Second edition [View all formats and editions](https://search.worldcat.org/formats-editions/967938939)
