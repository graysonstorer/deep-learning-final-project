# Field Listing: Area

Field Listing **::**Area

This entry includes three subfields. _Total area_ is the sum of all land and water areas delimited by international boundaries and/or coastlines. _Land area_ is the aggregate of all surfaces delimited by international boundaries and/or coastlines, excluding inland water bodies (lakes, reservoirs, rivers). _Water area_ is the sum of the surfaces of all inland water bodies, such as lakes, reservoirs, or rivers, as delimited by international boundaries and/or coastlines.
