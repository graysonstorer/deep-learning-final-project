Race and Ethnicity in the United States: 2010 Census and 2020 Census
====================================================================

 August 12, 2021 

The 2020 Census data underlying this visualization are available in the[Redistricting Data Summary Files](https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/decennial-census/about/rdo/summary-files.html#P1). We’re currently preparing easier-to-use tables for[data.census.gov](https://www.census.gov/data/data-tools/data-cedsci.html), scheduled for release in September. Changes since 2010 can be calculated using the[2010 Census data](https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/decennial-census/about/rdo/summary-files.2010.html#par_textimage).
