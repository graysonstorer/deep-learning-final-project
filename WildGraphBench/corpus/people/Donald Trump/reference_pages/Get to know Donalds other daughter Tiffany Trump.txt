![Image 2: Tiffany Trump walks the convention floor during the second day of the 2016 Republican National Convention.](https://usatoday.com/gcdn/media/2016/07/19/USATODAY/USATODAY/636045573711316711-AFP-554140238.jpg?crop=4000,3031,x0,y0&width=660&height=501&format=pjpg&auto=webp)
She‚Äôs Donald Trump‚Äôs daughter, the younger and less visible one after her half-sister Ivanka Trump became a fixture behind their dad‚Äôs podiums.

![Image 3](https://usatoday.com/story/news/politics/onpolitics/2016/07/19/who-is-tiffany-trump/87321708/)

She was raised in California by Trump‚Äôs second wife
---------------------------------------------------

Trump‚Äôs most visible children on the campaign trail ‚Äî Ivanka, Eric and Donald Jr. ‚Äî all come from his relationship with his first wife, Ivana Trump. Tiffany Trump comes from his relationship to his second wife, Marla Maples, a once-aspiring actress.

Maples has been seen more recently [on _Dancing with the Stars_](https://usatoday.com/story/life/tv/2016/03/08/trump-ex-wife-joins-dancing-stars/81465330/) as well as on the desk of ex-husband Donald Trump:

Tiffany was born two months before her parents‚Äô wedding in 1993, as [NBC notes](http://www.nbcnews.com/politics/politics-news/tiffany-trump-5-things-know-ahead-rnc-speech-n612566). After the couple divorced in 1999, Maples raised Tiffany in California ‚Äî the opposite side of the country from Trump‚Äôs New York empire.

"Since I have grown up on the West Coast, it definitely is different than all of them growing up on the East Coast," Tiffany said of her siblings in [a 2011 Oprah segment](http://www.oprah.com/oprahshow/meet-tiffany-trump-video).

"It's a different lifestyle, obviously, California vs. New York. (In) New York, my dad is like the center of the city."
