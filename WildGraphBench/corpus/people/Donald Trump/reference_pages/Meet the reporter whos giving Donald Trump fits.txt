By [Chris Cillizza](https://www.washingtonpost.com/people/chris-cillizza/)

 and

[David A. Fahrenthold](https://www.washingtonpost.com/people/david-a-fahrenthold/)

_WaPo's[David Fahrenthold](https://twitter.com/fahrenthold)is riding the hottest story in politics: The Trump Foundation. David has done massive amounts of reporting that make clear that [Trump uses almost none of his own money to make donations from the foundation](https://www.washingtonpost.com/politics/how-donald-trump-retooled-his-charity-to-spend-other-peoples-money/2016/09/10/da8cce64-75df-11e6-8149-b8d05321db62\_story.html). I reached out to him to walk through how he happened onto the story, the novel way he has pursued it and what the reaction to the pieces has been. Our conversation, conducted via email and only lightly edited, is below._
