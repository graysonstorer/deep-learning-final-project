IV. REPORTED INCIDENTS 

A. Landslide Incidents 

 Catbalogan, Samar 

o A landslide occurred at about 8:30 AM, March 20, at Brgy. 8, Catbalogan, Samar, damaging three (3) houses 

o Concerned Brgy. officials warned the neighboring residents to vacate the area but the residents refused to. The local PNP cordoned the area.

V. PREPAREDNESS/ RESPONSE MEASURES: 

National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council Operations Center (NDRRMC OpCen)  

> •

NDRRMC Emergency OpCen was activated at “BLUE ALERT” STATUS to coordinate all efforts in response to the possible effects of the present LPA and to continuously provide advisories/warnings/bulletins and other related information to all concerned  

> •

Disseminated NDRRMC Weather Advisories on Low Pressure Area (LPA), Gale Warnings, and Flood Bulletins to all regions likely to be affected through SMS and facsimile and uploaded the same at the NDRRMC Website, Facebook, and Twitter accounts for further dissemination to their respective local Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Councils (LDRRMCs) from the provincial down to the municipal levels  

> •

Directed RDRRMCs concerned through the OCD Regional Centers to undertake precautionary measures in their respective areas of responsibility (AOR) and subsequently advised local DRRMCs to initiate pre-emptive evacuation of families in low-lying and mountainous areas if situation warrants
