[Ten-Day Regional Agri-Weather Information](http://www.pagasa.dost.gov.ph/index.php/agriculture/climate-information-for-agriculture/ten-day-regional-agri-weather-information)

DECADE NO. 36 December 21- 31, 2016

**PHILIPPINE AGRI-WEATHER FORECAST******The weather systems that will affect the whole country are northeast monsoon, tail-end of a cold front, easterlies and tropical cyclone.

 During the early days of the decade, cloudy skies with light to moderate rains are expected over Visayas, Northern Mindanao and Palawan. Afterwards, Bicol Region, Eastern Visayas, Mindanao, CALABARZON, Central Luzon and provinces of Mindoro will experience moderate to heavy rains due to the possible development of tropical cyclone inside the Philippine Area of Responsibility. Meanwhile, cloudy skies with light to moderate rains are expected over the rest Luzon. The rest of the country will have partly cloudy to cloudy skies with isolated rainshowers and thunderstorms.

 Northeasterly winds will prevail throughout the decade.

 The coastal waters over Luzon and eastern section of Visayas will be moderate to rough while slight to moderate seas over the rest of the country.

The first tropical cyclone of the year starts with the name beginning in letter A as in AMBO under column 1 for 2016 and so on down the list as one disturbance succeeds another. The 5th year (2020) will bring us back to column 1 of AMBO. In the event that the number of tropical cyclones within the year exceeds 25, an auxiliary list is used, the first ten of which are listed under each column.
