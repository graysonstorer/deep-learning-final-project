**Newsbrief:**In an unusual bit of cross-promotion, Steam is now selling high-definition digital copies of all four _Mad Max_ movies - though the recently released _Fury Road_ is only available for pre-purchase - alongside Avalanche Studio's new _Max Max_ video game.

The movies and game will be offered in [a variety of packages](http://store.steampowered.com/bundle/65/), and will be available to Steam users based in the US, Canada, Russia, Brazil, South Korea and most of Europe.

It's a curious new direction for the digital retailer, which, until now, has only ever [sold a handful of video game-related movies and documentaries](http://store.steampowered.com/tag/en/Movie/#os%5B%5D=mac&p=0&tab=NewReleases).
