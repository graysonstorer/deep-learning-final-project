83: RSS](http://www.gamasutra.com/images/icon_rss.gif)](http://feeds.feedburner.com/GamasutraNews) **September 6, 2011** ![Image 84: Valve's Steam Trading Leaves Beta, Adds Portal 2 Support](http://www.gamasutra.com/db_area/images/news2001/37055/valve.jpg) ![Image 85: A 300x19 small image, likely a logo, icon or avatar](http://www.gamasutra.com/images/advertisement_header.gif) A new feature for users of Valve's popular Steam service that allows in-game items and gifts to be traded went live Tuesday. The feature, called Steam Trading, started an [open beta](http://www.gamasutra.com/view/news/36476/Valve_Introduces_Steam_Trading_Beta.php) about four weeks ago with support for the company's _Team Fortress 2_. Today's [public release](http://store.steampowered.com/news/6201/) has added support for another popular Valve title, _Portal 2_, as well as a third-party title, Three Rings and Sega's _Spiral Knights_. According to Valve, over 1 million virtual items were traded or gifted during the beta period. Valve introduced a popular virtual item storefront for _Team Fortress 2_[last year](http://www.gamasutra.com/view/news/30716/Team_Fortress_2_Introduces_Virtual_Item_Buys.php).
