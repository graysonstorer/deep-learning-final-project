by [Ross Miller](http://www.joystiq.com/editor/ross-miller)[![Image 31: A 11x10 small image, likely a logo, icon or avatar](http://www.blogsmithmedia.com/www.joystiq.com/media/writer_rss.gif)](http://www.joystiq.com/editor/ross-miller/rss.xml) on Sep 13th 2007 1:30PM

[27](http://www.joystiq.com/2007/09/13/steam-community-officially-launched#)

[![Image 32](http://www.blogcdn.com/www.joystiq.com/media/2007/09/steam-comm-welcome.jpg)](https://steamcommunity.com/)

 Valve has officially launched its [Steam Community](https://steamcommunity.com/), a system [akin](http://www.steampowered.com/steamtour/4.php) to Xbox Live where you can check fellow player's stats, plan group events and access the community information from within any Steam game. Also available both in and out of game is voice chat.

The service is free addition to Steam users. Tell us what you think of it in the never-ending comment space below.[](http://www.joystiq.com/2007/09/13/steam-community-officially-launched)
