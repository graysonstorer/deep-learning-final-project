WebMoney Now Available on Steam
===============================

 Announcement - Valve 

Dec 17, 2010

 Steam welcomes WebMoney, our newest payment method, now available for use in many countries when you [shop on the Steam store](http://store.steampowered.com/)!

Countries include: Russian Federation, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Belarus, Latvia, Estonia, Lithuania, Kyrgyzstan, Azerbaijan, Israel, Germany, Spain, Poland, Thailand, Czech Republic, France, Turkey, Switzerland, United Arab Emirates, Finland, and Singapore.
