84: RSS](http://twimgs.com/gamasutra/images/icon_rss.gif)](http://feeds.feedburner.com/GamasutraNews) **May 2, 2012** ![Image 85: A 300x19 small image, likely a logo, icon or avatar](http://twimgs.com/gamasutra//images/advertisement_header.gif) **Newsbrief**: The latest beta client build for Valve's Steam digital distribution platform adds remote management functionality, allowing users to download and install games to a home computer from a remote source. Registered Steam users can access the official Steam website from a remote computer or mobile device and manage their game library. If a home computer is logged into the Steam client, it's then possible to download and install Steam games onto the computer remotely.
