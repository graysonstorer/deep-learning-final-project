Valve adds concept section to Greenlight
========================================

A minor update for Greenlight allows users to distinguish between idea pitches and finished games.

![Image 5: steam greenlight](http://images.vg247.com/current//2012/08/steam-greenlight.jpg)

The spotlight has been on Valve’s latest addition to its digital distribution platform. Many small developers were disappointed with the $100 charge of pitching their projects. Valve has now ensured concepts are added without the fee.

Expect the platform to receive a number of minor changes in the coming weeks. Although finished products will receive the same deal as before, concept votes are now for developer eyes only.

Thanks, [Develop](http://www.develop-online.net/news/42278/Valve-adds-Greenlight-concept-section).
