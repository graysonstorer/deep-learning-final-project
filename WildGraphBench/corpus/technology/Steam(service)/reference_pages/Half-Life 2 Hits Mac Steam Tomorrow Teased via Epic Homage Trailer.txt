**[Update 4:47 PM - BL]**Valve has confirmed to Shacknews that this release includes Half-Life 2, Half-Life 2: Episode 1 and Half-Life 2: Episode 2.
**[Original Story]**Gordon Freeman's epic adventure through City 17 will hit the Mac version of Steam sometime tomorrow. To tease this, Valve has released an homage trailer to the [landmark 1984 Apple Macintosh commercial](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OYecfV3ubP8).

[Shackvideo users](http://www.shackvideo.com/) can use the [HD Stream](http://www.shackvideo.com/portal-2-free-yourself-teaser-trailer-18902).
I've reached out to Valve to find out if this is just the original release of Half-Life 2 or if it will include Episodes 1 and 2.
