# Thor: Love and Thunder's Tessa Thompson explains Valkyrie's big change

With _[Thor: Love and Thunder](https://www.digitalspy.com/movies/a27237155/thor-4-release-date-cast-trailer-plot-villain-spoilers/)_'s upcoming release, [Tessa Thompson](https://www.digitalspy.com/movies/a38792605/thor-love-and-thunder-tessa-thompson-valkyrie-powers/) has opened up about the major new change for her character Valkyrie.

In _Ragnarok_, Valkyrie was introduced as a warrior turned scrapper who loved a drink and was the Grandmaster's favourite for finding prized champions. By the end of _Avengers: Endgame_, she is King of Asgard as Thor gives her the throne.

Thompson admitted that in _Love and Thunder_, Valkyrie now has a job she "loves", looking after the kingdom, but is "disgruntled" as she's stuck on the sidelines away from the battlefield.

![Image 1: tessa thompson, thor love and thunder](https://hips.hearstapps.com/hmg-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/images/tessa-thompson-thor-love-and-thunder-1655999265.jpg?resize=480:*)

Marvel Studios

**Related:****[_Thor: Love and Thunder_'s first reactions have arrived](https://www.digitalspy.com/movies/a40391357/thor-love-and-thunder-first-reactions/)**

"With this one, we also talked about the idea of someone that has a job that they really love, but they're also kind of disgruntled," she told **Digital Spy** and other outlets at a recent press conference.

"She was a professional soldier for thousands of years and now finds herself kind of stuck in bureaucracy."

However, Thompson teased that Valkyrie finds a new kick-ass sisterhood with [Jane Foster (Natalie Portman)](https://www.digitalspy.com/movies/a39933182/thor-love-and-thunder-new-look-natalie-portman/).

"She's really missing being on the battlefield and missing her sisters," Thompson added. "It's been great fun to get to have that again, with Natalie in particular as Mighty Thor."

![Image 2: tessa thompson as king valkyrie and natalie portman as mighty thor in thor love and thunder](https://hips.hearstapps.com/hmg-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/images/tessa-thompson-natalie-portman-thor-love-and-thunder-1652861295.jpg?resize=480:*)

Thompson also reflected on how Valkyrie's narrative has been impacted by the trauma of losing her legion of warriors in battle against Hela (Cate Blanchett).

"The thing that I've really enjoyed about these films and certainly I think something that Stan Lee talked a lot about, whether it's a villain or a hero, the thing sometimes that connects them to their power, whether it's used for good or bad, is actually their trauma," she began.

"When we first met Valkyrie [in _Ragnarok_], she had a tremendous amount of it, and she was dealing with that by drinking a lot."

Thompson revealed that she worked closely with director Taika Waititi to reinvent the nature of a female superhero for Valkyrie.

![Image 3: valkyrie, tessa thompson, thor ragnarok](https://hips.hearstapps.com/digitalspyuk.cdnds.net/17/26/tessa-thompson-valkyrie-thor-ragnarok.jpg?resize=480:*)

Marvel Studios

**Related:****[_Thor: Love and Thunder_ star Christian Bale didn't know what MCU was before he joined film](https://www.digitalspy.com/movies/a40370643/thor-love-and-thunder-christian-bale-didnt-know-mcu/)**

"For Taika and I, we talked a lot about, like, upending what a female superhero looks like," she explained.

"That you have this moment where she comes out, and you think it’s gonna be sort of that badass thing and then, she immediately falls over.

"That was really fun to both try to inhabit the spirit and the physicality that it takes to be a hero, which is its own skill, and also to allow her to be fun and sort of goofy."
