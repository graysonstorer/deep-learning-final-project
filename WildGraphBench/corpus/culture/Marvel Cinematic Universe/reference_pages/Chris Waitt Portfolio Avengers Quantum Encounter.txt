# Chris Waitt Portfolio – Avengers: Quantum Encounter

Chris directed ****Paul Rudd****and****Evangeline Lilly****as their Marvel characters **Ant-Man** and **Wasp** in over twenty minutes of content, including comedy and action sequences,in what has been described as"something that’s never been done before in a restaurant on land or at sea!" Chris worked with the writers and creators at**Disney Imagineering**and Oscar winning company **Framestore** on this **ground-breaking experiential content**for the forthcoming**Disney Wish**.
