# Chris Waitt Portfolio – Web Slingers: A Spider-Man Adventure

Working with the brilliant team at Disney Imagineering, and alongside Spider-Man VFX supervisor Doug Bloom, Chris creatively directed Framestore’s work on the elaborate and hilarious pre-show film which has **Tom Holland** accidentally set off a hoard of replicating Spider-Bots which kicks off the **Web Slingers: A Spider-Man Adventure** where guests wrangle the rogue Spider-Bots as they run amok through the **Avengers Campus**.

**Web Slingers: A Spider-Man Adventure**is the main attraction of the Avengers Campus which is the newest addition to Disneyland California.
