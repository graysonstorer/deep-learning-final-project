# Zoe Saldaña teases Guardians of the Galaxy 3's emotional story

_[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol 3](https://www.digitalspy.com/movies/a829022/guardians-of-the-galaxy-3-trailer-cast-release-date/)_ star Zoe Saldaña says the [Marvel](https://www.digitalspy.com/marvel) sequel's "beautiful story" frequently leaves the cast feeling "emotional" during filming, as they prepare to say goodbye to the titular team.

Just last month, writer-director [James Gunn confirmed](https://www.digitalspy.com/movies/a38921731/guardians-of-the-galaxy-3-james-gunn-team-ending/) that the third movie will mark the end of the road for this particular band of space-based misfits, which currently consists of Saldaña's Gamora, Pom Klementieff's Mantis, [Dave Bautista's Drax](https://www.digitalspy.com/movies/a36330004/dave-bautista-drax-guardians-of-the-galaxy-3-future/), Vin Diesel's Groot, Bradley Cooper's Rocket, Karen Gillan's Nebula and Chris Pratt's Peter Quill/Star-Lord.

![Image 1: guardians of the galaxy 2 zoe saldana as gamora](https://hips.hearstapps.com/digitalspyuk.cdnds.net/16/42/1476894614-guardians-of-the-galaxy-2-gamora.jpg?crop=0.621xw:1.00xh;0.170xw,0&resize=480:*)

Marvel Studios

**Related:****[James Gunn teases unexpected _Guardians of the Galaxy 3_](https://www.digitalspy.com/movies/a38803755/guardians-of-the-galaxy-3-james-gunn-unexpected/)**

Whether or not some of them will remain a part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe is unclear. It's certain, though, that the gang won't all be seen clambering into the Milano again beyond _Vol 3 –_ a fact that Saldaña describes as "bittersweet".

"After all those years of complaining about that green make-up, I find myself already nostalgic about it," she admitted to _[Empire](https://www.empireonline.com/)_ magazine recently. "There's a lot of melancholy here, but also pride that we've achieved something great. James Gunn has written a beautiful story, that's making us emotional even as we shoot it.

"Omicron plays a doozy on us every other day, but we're getting by and we're happy."

When asked whether her relationship with Gamora has changed since she brought her to life for the first time back in 2014, noting that she's since played versions of her in _Vol 2_, _Avengers: Infinity War_ and _[Avengers: Endgame](https://www.digitalspy.com/the-avengers/)_, Saldaña confessed: "Oh my god, yes.

![Image 2: gamora, avengers infinity war](https://hips.hearstapps.com/hmg-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/images/gamora-avengers-infinity-war-1556117108.jpg?resize=480:*)

Disney

****Related: [Dwayne Johnson's Christmas movie compared to _Guardians of the Galaxy_](https://www.digitalspy.com/movies/a38703355/dwayne-johnson-christmas-movie-guardians-of-galaxy/)****

"She began as this warrior wanting to get out of a very toxic parental relationship, then she finds the Guardians, who are dysfunctional-ish as well, but they're okay. She learns a lot and finds this bigger purpose," she went on.

"Then her dad comes and f**ks everything up. She dies, she's brought back, but it's not her. I think Gamora's a little confused, I have to say! But James has weaved this wonderful story around the character, and every character in this third film."

Outside of the Guardians, _Vol 3_ is expected to see Elizabeth Debicki reprise her role as Ayesha, the Golden High Priestess of the Sovereign, while [_Black Mirror_'s Will Poulter will be introduced as Adam Warlock](https://www.digitalspy.com/movies/a38012657/marvel-guardians-of-the-galaxy-vol-3-will-poulter/), the being she created at the end of the second movie.
