Alfre Woodard's Captain America: Civil War Character Is Different Than Her Luke Cage Role
=========================================================================================

Share.

 Jim Rash also cast in the Marvel film. 

By [Sasha Erfanian](http://people.ign.com/Azazel_Jones)The guest list for next week's U.S. premiere of [Captain America: Civil War](https://www.ign.com/movies/captain-america-civil-war "Captain America: Civil War") briefly raised hopes that Marvel could be uniting its film and Netflix universes through the inclusion of an actor no one knew was in the movie, Oscar nominee Alfre Woodard.

![Image 31: Alfre Woodard in State of Affairs](http://oyster.ignimgs.com/wordpress/stg.ign.com/2015/08/aflrewoodard-720x405.jpg)

[Alfre Woodard](https://www.ign.com/stars/alfre-woodard "Alfre Woodard") in State of Affairs

We've known for a while that Woodard (True Blood, the animated Black Panther series) [had been cast in Luke Cage](http://ca.ign.com/articles/2015/08/21/alfre-woodard-joins-marvels-luke-cage-netflix-series) as "Minetta," a powerful local politician who may be a version of the villain Black Mariah.

However, [ScreenRant reports that her role in Civil War is completely separate](http://screenrant.com/captain-america-3-alfre-woodard-luke-cage/). According to their sources, she'll be playing the grieving mother of someone who died in Avengers: Age of Ultron's climactic final battle who convinces Tony Stark to support the film's version of the Superhero Registration Act.

![Image 32: Beyond](https://www.ign.com/articles/2016/04/06/alfre-woodards-captain-america-civil-war-character-is-different-than-her-luke-cage-role)

[Captain America: Civil War - Trailer #2](http://www.ign.com/videos/2016/3/10/captain-america-civil-war-trailer-2-ign-pt)

The premiere's guest list also revealed that Community's [Jim Rash](https://www.ign.com/stars/jim-rash "Jim Rash") has a role in Captain America: Civil War. The film's directors, Joe and Anthony Russo, directed many episodes of Community, and gave cast member Danny Pudi a small role in their previous Marvel movie, Captain America: The Winter Soldier.

While The Defenders won't be assembling with The Avengers just yet, you can get a sneak peak [of Luke Cage's first season right now](http://ca.ign.com/articles/2016/03/19/luke-cage-how-you-can-see-the-new-teaser-trailer). Plus, see where[the Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. land on the Civil War's battle lines](http://ca.ign.com/articles/2016/03/22/where-the-agents-of-shield-side-in-marvels-civil-war) ahead of the movie's release on May 6.![Image 33: IGN Logo](http://oystatic.ignimgs.com/src/core/img/widgets/global/page/globhead_dpad_red.png)
