Marvel to Celebrate 75 Years With 1-Hour Primetime Special on ABC

Original primetime TV special hosted by Emily VanCamp to celebrate Marvel’s history!

#### Comments:

2014 marks Marvel’s 75th anniversary, and as part of the celebrations fans can catch an original, hour-long primetime special of our full history from 1939 to present, plus an extraordinary peek into Marvel's future!

“Marvel 75 Years: From Pulp to Pop!” will take fans new and old alike through the decades with the people who lived them in exclusive interviews,hosted by Emily VanCamp, star of ABC’s “Revenge” and Marvel’s “Captain America: The Winter Soldier.”

Tune in Tuesday, November 4 at 9:00 p.m. ET on ABC to re-live 75 years of your favorite heroes in “Marvel 75 Years: From Pulp to Pop!,” and stay tuned to[Marvel.com](http://marvel.com/)for more on the stupendous special, True Believers!
