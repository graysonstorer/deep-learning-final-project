# Daredevil: Born Again's Vincent D'Onofrio shares strange Kingpin tease

Ahead of his return to the _[Daredevil](https://www.digitalspy.com/daredevil/)_-verse in new series _[Born Again](https://www.digitalspy.com/tv/ustv/a40704403/daredevil-born-again-series-marvel/)_, star Vincent D'Onofrio has shared a rather… strange tease.

The Kingpin actor is due to return for the upcoming [Marvel](https://www.digitalspy.com/marvel/) reboot, which will stream on [Disney+](https://www.digitalspy.com/disney/) following _Daredevil'_ s cancellation in 2018.

Taking to his Twitter page yesterday (September 1), D'Onofrio shared a picture of a flask with the work "Fisk" written on it.

What could that mean?

One fan theory suggests it's something to do with a Super Soldier Serum, which will revive his character Wilson Fisk after a [presumed bullet wound](https://www.digitalspy.com/tv/ustv/a38626225/hawkeye-vincent-donofrio-kingpin-return/) at the hands of Maya Lopez (Alaqua Cox) at the end of _[Hawkeye](https://www.digitalspy.com/hawkeye/)._

The actor is set to be joined by fellow star Charlie Cox, who plays Matt Murdock, in _Daredevil: Born Again_, which is expected to consist of 18 episodes and premiere in 2024.

Before that, they are both set to appear in series _Echo_, which will focus on the character of Maya Lopez from _Hawkeye_.

Cox is also back as Daredevil in current Marvel series _[She-Hulk: Attorney at Law](https://www.digitalspy.com/she-hulk/)_, with show boss Kat Coiro [teasing a more "playful" side to the character](https://www.digitalspy.com/tv/ustv/a40896784/she-hulk-daredevil-mcu-return/) for fans.

"I can definitely tease you, but I can't say anything! There are lots of surprises," she said. "Daredevil, you know, I think we'll get to see a more playful side of him than we've ever seen."

Meanwhile, fans were recently [given their first look at Kingpin's return](https://www.digitalspy.com/tv/ustv/a40859991/marvel-echo-first-look-kingpin-return/) in _Echo_ in a picture from filming, showing the character back in a white suit.
