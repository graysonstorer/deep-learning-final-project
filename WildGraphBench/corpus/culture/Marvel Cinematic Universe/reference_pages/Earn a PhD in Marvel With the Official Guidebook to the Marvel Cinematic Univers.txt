Earn a PhD in Marvel With the Official 'Guidebook to the Marvel Cinematic Universe' Series
==========================================================================================

Finally ready to admit how overwhelming the Marvel Cinematic Universe can be for a newcomer? Even Marvel understands.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Trace William Cowen](https://www.complex.com/author/tracewilliamcowen)

Updated on January 4, 2025

Share ADD COMPLEX ON GOOGLE 

![Image 1: Not Available Lead](https://images.complex.com/complex/image/upload/q_auto,f_auto,c_fill,ar_1.78,w_2048,g_auto/v1723881230/sanity-new/not-available-lead-133179668.jpg)

Image via Complex Original

Complex Original

The complex narrative structure at the center of the**Marvel Cinematic Universe**, in all its intersectional glory, can be a bit daunting for both newcomers and seasoned **Marvel** aficionados alike. As a subtle nod to its own classic _Official Handbook to the Marvel Universe_ series, Marvel has just announced the official _Guidebook to the Marvel Cinematic Universe_ in an effort to further educate future Marvel scholars in the most academically immersive way possible.

Each entry in this sleek handbook series features a smorgasbord of fast facts, movie-to-comic comparisons, and production stills from instaclassic Marvel films. The series kicks off in October with the release of _Guidebook to the Marvel Cinematic Universe: Marvel's Iron Man_ (see above), detailing the rise of **Tony Stark** within the blockbuster context of the 2008 film starring **Robert Downey Jr.**_Marvel's Iron Man_ is followed quickly by the release of the stacked flipbook _Guidebook to the Marvel Cinematic Universe: Marvel's Incredible Hulk / Marvel's Iron Man 2_ in November, just in time for that relative you only see once a year to pick it up for you as some sort of holiday amends:

With cover art from**Mike Del Mundo**and **Pascal Campion** pairing brilliantly with a scholarly breakdown from **Mike O'Sullivan**and the **Ohotmu Team**, these MCU guidebooks are clearly necessary additions to one's personal library.
