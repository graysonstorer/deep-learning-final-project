[Lego Marvel's Avengers](http://www.ew.com/topic/lego-marvels-avengers)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Posted October 12 2015 — 11:10 AM EDT

When _Lego Marvel’s Avengers_ was announced, the latest entry in TT Games’ long-running Lego series looked to be a case of pairing down scope. But the new trailer, and a talk with the developers, suggests the exact opposite is true, as the developer hopes to outdo itself with it’s latest marvelous outing.

### Related

[![Image 4](http://www.ew.com/sites/default/files/styles/tout_image_196x134/public/1433872181/Screen%20Shot%202015-06-09%20at%201.46.56%20PM.png?itok=lbXc--QO) 'Lego Avengers' trailer shows off battle of New York, hints at Ultron](http://www.ew.com/article/2015/06/09/lego-marvel-avengers-e3-trailer)

[![Image 5](http://www.ew.com/sites/default/files/styles/tout_image_196x134/public/i/2015/04/23/avengers-age-of-ultron-review_0.jpg?itok=D1-SO__l) See the honest trailer for 'Avengers: Age of Ultron'](http://www.ew.com/article/2015/09/29/avengers-age-ultron-honest-trailer)

A new trailer for the game debuted at New York Comic Con, revealing that _Lego Marvel’s Avengers_ is not just going to focus on the two big-screen _Avengers_ outings. As the trailer indicates, there will be story content covering much of Phase 2 and even some of Phase 1 of the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

The game will include story content for both _Avengers_ films, both _Captain America_ films,_Thor: The Dark World_, and _Iron Man 3_. The developers also revealed that, in addition to using some of the voice work from the film, Marvel stalwarts like Clark Gregg, Cobie Smulders, and Hayley Atwell were brought in for additional voicework. These additions include missions centered around _Agent Carter_ in the game’s New York hub world, which transforms the brightly colored streets of Manhattan into a black-and-white, 1940’s era version.

The game’s developers also told EW that while the focus is of course on the heroes’ cinematic outings, TT Games is fitting in as many characters, artwork, and other nods to the characters’ comic book origins. Check out the new look at the game, which is arriving January 26 for the PlayStation 4, PlayStation 3, PlayStation Vita, Xbox One, Xbox 360, Windows PC, Wii U, and Nintendo 3DS.
