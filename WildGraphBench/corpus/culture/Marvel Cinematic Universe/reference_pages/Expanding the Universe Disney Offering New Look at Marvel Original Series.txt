Expanding the Universe: Disney+ Offering New Look at Marvel Original Series
===========================================================================

*   by [Samuel Gelman](https://www.cbr.com/author/sgel/)
*   – on Nov 11, 2019
*   in [TV](https://www.cbr.com/category/tv/), [TV News](https://www.cbr.com/category/news-tv/)

![Image 3](https://www.cbr.com/expanding-the-universe-disney-marvel-original-series/)

[Disney+](https://www.cbr.com/tag/disney-plus/)will offer a special look at its upcoming Marvel TV shows when the streaming service debuts on Nov. 12.

Titled _Expanding the Universe_, the special will provide viewers with a sneak peek at upcoming TV series like _The Falcon and the Winter Solider_ and _Wandavision_. It will be available as soon as Disney+ launches.

RELATED:[Falcon & Winter Soldier Star Shares Photo From First Day of Filming](https://www.cbr.com/falcon-winter-soldier-star-shares-photo-from-first-day-of-filming/)

> From Iron Man to Endgame, see the newly announced [@MarvelStudios](https://twitter.com/MarvelStudios?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw) titles that will be available to stream starting tomorrow on [#DisneyPlus](https://twitter.com/hashtag/DisneyPlus?src=hash&ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw)—including Expanding the Universe, a new look at the original series coming to the service.
> 
> 
> 
> Look out for more titles to be announced soon.
> 
> — Disney+ (@disneyplus) [November 11, 2019](https://twitter.com/disneyplus/status/1193985534927892480?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw)

Aside from the new special, Disney+ also announced all the Marvel films that will be available at launch. The list includes _Iron Man, Iron Man 2, Thor, Captain America:The First Avenger, The Avengers, Iron Man 3, Thor: The Dark World, Captain America: The Winter Soldier, Guardians of the Galaxy, Avengers: Age of Ultron, Ant-Man, Captain American: Civil War, Doctor Strange,Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2, Captain Marvel_ and _Avengers: Endgame_. More titles are expected to be announced soon.

[![Image 4](https://www.cbr.com/expanding-the-universe-disney-marvel-original-series/) Start now](https://www.cbr.com/expanding-the-universe-disney-marvel-original-series/tldr/1)

Disney+ currently has several Marvel TV shows in development aside from the above mentioned, including _Loki,Hawkeye, Ms. Marvel, Moon Knight, She-Hulk_ and _What If...?_

Directed by Kari Skogland, _Falcon and the Winter Soldier_ stars Anthony Mackie, Sebastian Stan, Emily VanCamp, Wyatt Russell and Daniel Brühl and will be available on Disney+. The series will hit the streaming service in Fall 2020.
