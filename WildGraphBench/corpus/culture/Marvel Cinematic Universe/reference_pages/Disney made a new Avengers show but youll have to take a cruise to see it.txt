_Avengers: Quantum Encounter_ is the newest Marvel team-up, but it’s not going to be easy to see. The new story will be an experience exclusive to the new the new [Wish cruise ship](https://disneycruise.disney.go.com/why-cruise-disney/wish/), [Disney announced on Tuesday](https://disneyparks.disney.go.com/blog/2021/10/stars-of-the-marvel-cinematic-universe-assemble-for-first-of-its-kind-dining-adventure-aboard-the-disney-wish/).

As you may have guessed [from the name](https://www.polygon.com/2020/9/14/21436481/ant-man-3-cast-marvel-mcu-kang-conqueror-jonathan-majors), _Quantum Encounter_ focuses on [Ant-Man and The Wasp](https://www.polygon.com/2018/7/16/17578170/ant-man-and-the-wasp-after-credits-scene-director-avengers-connections), who are giving a presentation on new Pym Tech that gets crashed by Ultron and an army of Robots. The story will star both Paul Rudd and Evangeline Lilly, who will team up with Brie Larson’s Captain Marvel and [Anthony Mackie](https://www.polygon.com/22631179/marvel-captain-america-4-release-date-anthony-mackie-avengers) in his first group outing as Captain America. Ross Marquand (_The Walking Dead_) will voice Ultron. There will also be a special surprise cameo from another hero, but we don’t know who that is just yet.

_Avengers: Quantum Encounter_ will be an integral part of Disney’s new Wish cruise ship. The show will take place during dinner and will include interactable sections for diners, as well as hundreds of screens and specially designed effects to enhance the show and the dining experience. The show also includes a full CGI re-creation of the Wish’s upper decks, which is where most of the battles in it take place.

_Avengers: Quantum Encounter_ has apparently finished filming and will be ready for the Wish’s launch in summer 2022.
