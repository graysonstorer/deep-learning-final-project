If you're a big fan of Marvel and the type of person who gets over-excited at the prospect of Disneyland and wearing the obligatory Mickey Mouse ears, then listen up!

Thursday (January 12) saw the grand opening of the first ever Marvel-themed ride at Disneyland.

The Iron Man Experience at Hong Kong's Disney park is the first of many planned Marvel expansions by Disney and allows fans to finally immerse themselves in the world of Stark Industries.

©Getty Images

_[Iron Man is joined by Walt Disney Parks chairman Bob Chapek, Marvel Studios president Kevin Feige and Marvel Entertainment chief creative officer Joe Quesada_ _]_

A Disney press release stated: "Anchoring a unique storyline, the eminent innovator and pioneer Tony Stark has selected Tomorrowland in Hong Kong Disneyland to host Stark Expo, where he will show off his latest high-tech creations in various exhibition halls – the Hall of Legacy, the Hall of Protection, the Hall of Energy and the Hall of Mobility.

"Guests will be among the first to explore Tony Stark's latest innovations up close and fly in a gravity-defying Iron Wing flight vehicle in Iron Man Experience.

"He has also made Hong Kong the Asia headquarters of Stark Industries hereafter. The new attraction takes guests on the epic adventure of a lifetime as they join Iron Man in a battle against Hydra across the city of Hong Kong."

It adds: "Guests will make their way to the Hong Kong Stark Tower powered by the arc reactor and whizz alongside Iron Man through streets that resemble those in West Kowloon and downtown, and fly over iconic Hong Kong landmarks like Tsing Ma Bridge, Victoria Harbour and the city's pristine mountain ranges."

Let's hope it'll be hitting Florida or Paris sometime soon...
