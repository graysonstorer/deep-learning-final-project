Got time for a 30+ movie marathon?

The complete Marvel Cinematic Universe timeline is now available to stream on [Disney+](https://www.marvel.com/disneyplus). Whether you’re a longtime Marvel fan or just stepping into the MCU for the first time, you can visit the [Marvel brand page on Disney+](https://www.disneyplus.com/brand/marvel?cid=DTCI-Synergy-Marvel-Site-Acquisition-Originals-US-Marvel-Marvel-EN-ArticleLink-Marvel_ArticleLink_Marvel-NA) to see the **_MCU Movie Timeline_**and the **_MCU Complete Timeline,_**which includes films and TV series broken out by season.

Beginning with [_Eyes of Wakanda_](https://www.marvel.com/tv-shows/animation/eyes-of-wakanda/1)and[_Captain America: The First Avenger_](https://www.marvel.com/movies/captain-america-the-first-avenger)and up through [_Thunderbolts*_](https://www.marvel.com/movies/thunderbolts)[,](https://www.marvel.com/movies/captain-america-brave-new-world) there’s so much to discover in every corner of the galaxy — including this one (Earth), Asgard, Knowhere, Hala, and all universes in between.

Check out the Complete MCU Timeline with the TV seasons broken up below.
