NEW DELHI: The government informed the Rajya Sabha that 254 Hindus and 790 Muslims were killed in Gujarat in the post-Godhra riots of 2002.

![Image 3: 790 Muslims, 254 Hindus perished in post-Godhra](https://static.toiimg.com/thumb/msid-47529300,imgsize-110164,width-400,resizemode-4/47529300.jpg)

NEW DELHI: The government informed the Rajya Sabha that 254 Hindus and 790 Muslims were killed in Gujarat in the post-Godhra riots of 2002. 

Stating this in a written reply, minister of state for home affairs Sriprakash Jaiswal said a total of 223 people were reported missing, 2,548 sustained injuries, 919 were rendered widows and 606 children were orphaned during the riots.

The state government, he said has informed that a total of Rs 204.62 crore has been incurred by it towards relief and rehabilitation measures.
