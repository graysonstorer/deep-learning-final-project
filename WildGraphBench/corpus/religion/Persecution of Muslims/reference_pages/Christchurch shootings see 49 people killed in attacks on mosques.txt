Christchurch shootings leave 49 people dead after attacks on mosques, as it happened
====================================================================================

Updated about 7 hours ago Fri 15 Mar 2019, 2:11pm

[**Related Story:** Australian in custody after 49 people killed in Christchurch terror attack](https://www.abc.net.au/news/2019-03-15/christchurch-shooting-at-mosques-leads-to-multiple-fatalities/10904306)

[**Map:** New Zealand](https://www.google.com/maps/place/New%20Zealand/@-41,174,5z)

Forty-nine people are now known to have been killed in two armed attacks on Christchurch mosques, in what Jacinda Ardern labelled "one of New Zealand's darkest days".

Scott Morrison said an Australian man massacred worshippers in an act of "extremist right-wing terrorism" which was streamed on the internet.

Look back at how a city, a country and the world was left reeling after this terrorist attack in our live blog.
