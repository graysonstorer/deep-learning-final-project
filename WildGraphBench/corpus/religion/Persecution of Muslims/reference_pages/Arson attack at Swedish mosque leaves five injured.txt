----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Swedish police are treating a fire at a mosque in the town of Eskilstuna as arson.

Five people were reportedly injured in the attack, which happened on Thursday afternoon.

The mosque is located on the ground floor of a building. Up to 20 people were inside when the fire broke out.

No arrests have yet been made.
