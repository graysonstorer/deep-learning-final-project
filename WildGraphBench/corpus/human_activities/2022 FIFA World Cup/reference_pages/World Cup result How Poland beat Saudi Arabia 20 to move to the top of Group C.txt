Man of the Match
----------------

A simple choice and it is the Poland goalkeeper Wojciech Szczesny who is my man of the match.

It was his incredible double save at the end of the first half, first from the penalty spot and then the follow-up, which kept Poland in the lead.

Two clean sheets and nine saves, the most of any goalkeeper in the tournament is not a bad way to start the competition.
