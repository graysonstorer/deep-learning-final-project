#### Story outline

*   2022 FIFA World Cup will be played in Qatar in November and France will be aiming to defend its 2018 title.
*   A total of 32 teams will participate in the tournament across 64 matches over a period of 29 days.
*   Brazil, Belgium and Argentina are the favourites to clinch the title. 
*   This could be the last world cup for one of the all-time greats - Messi and Ronaldo.
*   Fifa World Cup Qatar 2022 will be broadcast on Sports18 and Sports18 HD.
*   Fifa World Cup Qatar 2022 will be streamed on the JioCinema app in India for free.
*   The World Cup final will be played on Sunday, Dec. 18 .

 Brazil coach Tite during training

Due to excessive heat, the biggest sporting event in the world will be played in the autumn for the first time. The 2022 [FIFA World Cup](https://web.archive.org/web/20221122154832/https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/fifa-world-cup-2018-in-russia) will be played in [Qatar](https://web.archive.org/web/20221122154832/https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/topic/qatar) in November as France will be aiming to defend its 2018 title, but it will be a huge task as the other football powerhouses like [Brazil](https://web.archive.org/web/20221122154832/https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/topic/brazil), Belgium and Argentina could pose a massive challenge. This will be a special world cup, which begins on Sunday, as fans have been expecting something tremendous on-field action after all the action off-the-field and controversies. This could be the last world cup for one of the all-time greats - Messi and Ronaldo. Hence, football lovers just can't wait for the action to start: 

1.   **How many teams are participating in the [Fifa World Cup Qatar](https://web.archive.org/web/20221122154832/https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/topic/fifa-world-cup-qatar) 2022?**

A total of 32 teams will participate in the tournament across 64 matches over a period of 29 days.
2.   **Which TV channel in India will broadcast Fifa [World Cup Qatar](https://web.archive.org/web/20221122154832/https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/topic/world-cup-qatar) 2022?**

Fifa World Cup Qatar 2022 will be broadcast on Sports18 and Sports18 HD.
3.   **How to watch or stream Qatar [FIFA World Cup 2022](https://web.archive.org/web/20221122154832/https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/topic/fifa-world-cup-2022) live in India?**

Fifa World Cup Qatar 2022 will be streamed on the JioCinema app in India. You can watch matches on the JioCinema website for free.
4.   **When will the first knockout match be played in Qatar FIFA World Cup 2022?**

The first knockout round kicks off on Dec. 3 and will be followed by the quarter-finals from Dec. 9 and the semi-finals from Dec. 13.

**Which are the eight groups in the tournament?

GROUP A:**Qatar (hosts), Ecuador, Senegal, Netherlands. 

**GROUP B:**England, Iran, [United States](https://web.archive.org/web/20221122154832/https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/topic/united-states), Wales. 

**GROUP C:**Argentina, Saudi Arabia, Mexico, Poland. 

**GROUP D:**France, Australia, Denmark, [Tunisia](https://web.archive.org/web/20221122154832/https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/topic/tunisia). 

**GROUP E:** Spain, Costa Rica, Germany, Japan. 

**GROUP F:**Belgium, Canada, Morocco, Croatia. 

**GROUP G:** Brazil, Serbia, Switzerland, Cameroon. 

**GROUP H:**Portugal, Ghana, Uruguay, South Korea. 

The group stage will be a round-robin format with each of the 32 teams playing three matches. Teams will get three points for a win and one for a draw. The top two teams in each group will advance to the last 16. 

The knockout rounds will feature one-off matches, which can go to extra time and then penalties to decide the winner. 

The third-place playoff between the two losing semi-finalists takes place on Dec. 17 at the Khalifa International Stadium. 

**When is the FIFA World Cup 2022 final?**The World Cup final will be played on Sunday, Dec. 18 which is Qatar National Day. The match will kick off at 1500 GMT (6 p.m. local time). 

**Which stadium is hosting the World Cup 2022 final?**

The 80,000 capacity Lusail Iconic Stadium, which is 15km north of central Doha and the largest venue for the tournament, will host the World Cup title clash. 

**Who are the favourites to win the World Cup 2022?**

According to bookmakers, five-times champions Brazil are the favourites to clinch the title.
