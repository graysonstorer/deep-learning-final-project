This year is **a World Cup year with a difference**. The month-long tournament has traditionally taken place after the domestic leagues in Europe have been completed, usually **between June and July**. But the **2022 edition will be taking place in Qatar** - the first time the event will be staged in the **Middle East**. However in Qatar, the **average maximum daytime temperature in July is around 42¬∞C** - that‚Äôs too hot to play football or even watch it, so organisers FIFA decided to move the competition from its traditional date to later in the calendar year.

That means it will have a direct impact on all of the major **European leagues** and also **UEFA‚Äôs three club competitions** - the **Champions League**, **Europa League** and **Conference League**. ### **Changes to the Champions League group stage calendar**

**November**and **December**, are generally key months in the **Champions League**as it is precisely **when the group stage is decided**. Every year, FIFA usually has an eight or nine-day window in mid-November for **international fixtures** - which causes a break in the Champions League group stage**after Matchday 4**. In other words, with the final two rounds of group games, Matchdays 5 and 6, left to play. This year, **Matchdays 5 and 6 have been brought forward**which means that the group stage will be completed **before the World Cup kicks off** in Qatar. The **draw for the Round of 16** stage will also be held earlier - on **7 November**.

The dates below show how the **2022/23 Champions League** will accommodate the **Qatar World Cup**:

#### **Champions League group stage**

**Matchday 1**: 6/7 September

**Matchday 2**: 13/14 September

**Matchday 3**: 4/5 October

**Matchday 4**: 11/12 October

**Matchday 5**: 25/26 October

**Matchday 6**: 1/2 November

**2022/23 Champions League Round of 16 draw:**Monday 7 November

#### **FIFA 2022 World Cup Qatar**

**Inaugural match: Qatar vs Ecuador**- Sunday 20 November

**Final:**Sunday 18 December

The latter stages of the **Champions League** won‚Äôt be affected - the tournament will resume in the New Year with the **Round of 16** to be played on **14/15/21/22 February** and **7/8/14/15 March 2023**.
