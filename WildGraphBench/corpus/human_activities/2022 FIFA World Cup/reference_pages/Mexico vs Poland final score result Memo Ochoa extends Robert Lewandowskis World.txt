# Mexico vs Poland final score, result: Memo Ochoa extends Robert Lewandowski's World Cup frustration

Mexico and Poland both missed the chance to exploit [Argentina's shock defeat by Saudi Arabia](https://web.archive.org/web/20221127014206/https://www.sportingnews.com/us/soccer/news/argentina-vs-saudi-arabia-world-cup-live-score-result/guldh6njdecvphcvfjki22ze) as their opening World Cup 2022 Group C encounter ended goalless.

Gerardo Martino's side had the bulk of possession and the better chances overall, but Poland might have sneaked victory only for star striker Robert Lewandowski to miss a second-half penalty.
