State-of-the-art facilities have been arranged for all teams during the World Cup...

One of the most fascinating aspects of the 2022 World Cup in Qatar is that there will not be any need for the participating teams to travel across the nation as their journey unfolds in the marquee tournament.

With all stadiums located within an hour's distance of each other, participating teams do not have to travel constantly and shift their training bases.

All 32 teams will have such a Team Base Camp (TBC) assigned to them. The Team Base Camp will be the headquarters of a participating team during the World Cup. It combines a dedicated training site with a suitable hotel or other form of accommodation.

Interestingly, 24 out of the 32 teams will be staying within a 10 km radius in Doha. Germany, Mexico, Portugal, Saudi Arabia, England, Denmark and Belgium are the teams who are staying the farthest from Doha.

Let us take a look at where the Team Base Camps are for every World Cup-qualified side during the event in Qatar later this year.
