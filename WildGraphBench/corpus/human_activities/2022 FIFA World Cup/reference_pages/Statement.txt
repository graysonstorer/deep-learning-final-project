### STATEMENT

24 / 02 / 22 Author: PZPN 

[![Image 3: STATEMENT](https://pzpn.pl/public/system/images/articles/9936/13790-mini-3.jpg?ts=6a544fb7c905cd6971c3b06aa665862c)](https://pzpn.pl/public/system/images/articles/9936/13790-zoom.jpg?ts=6a544fb7c905cd6971c3b06aa665862c "STATEMENT")

Based on the current alarming development in the conflict between Russia and Ukraine, including the security situation, the Football Associations of Poland (PZPN), Sweden (SvFF) and Czech Republic (FAČR) express their firm position that the playoff matches to qualify for the 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar, scheduled for 24 and 29 March 2022, should not be played in the territory of the Russian Federation.

* * *

![Image 4: Statement of The Football Associations of Poland, Sweden and Czech Republic on the conflict between Russia and Ukraine and their firm position that playoff matches for the 2022 FIFA World Cup should not be played in Russian territory, dated 24.02.2022 and addressed to FIFA General Secretary Fatma SAMOURA](https://pzpn.pl/public/system/images/articles/9936/13789-zoom.jpg)
