Transition - The first ten years : analysis and lessons for Eastern Europe and the former Soviet Union (English)
================================================================================================================

This study looks at lessons to be drawn from the ten-year experience of the transition countries in Eastern Europe and the former Soviet Union in the period 1991 to 2000. The World's Bank "world Development Report 1996: From Plan to Market" focused on the transition process during the first half of this period.
