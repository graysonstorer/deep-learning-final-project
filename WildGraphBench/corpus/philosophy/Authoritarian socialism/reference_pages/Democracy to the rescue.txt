Democracy to the rescue?
========================

Despite an epidemic of scandal, the region is making progress against a plague
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Share

![Image 19](https://www.economist.com/cdn-cgi/image/width=1424,quality=80,format=auto/sites/default/files/images/print-edition/20150314_AMP001_1.jpg)

Mar 12th 2015|BUENOS AIRES, LIMA, MEXICO CITY AND SÃO PAULO|10 min read

WHEN Dilma Rousseff, Brazil’s president, delivered a televised speech to mark International Women’s Day on March 8th, it was almost impossible to hear her in some districts. Thousands of middle-class Brazilians drowned her out by banging pots and pans, a traditional way to show dissent in neighbouring countries. Brazil’s _panelaço_ is new.
