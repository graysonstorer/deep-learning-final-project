Ancient Chinese inventions
==========================

Authors: [Yinke Deng](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=au=%22Deng%2C%20Yinke%22) (Author), [Pinxing Wang](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=au=%22Wang%2C%20Pinxing%22) (Translator)

Summary: China has given birth to numerous scientific and technological inventions, and for many centuries led the world in such innovations. Indeed, some of the most important inventions in the history of human civilization originated in China, not least the compass, gunpowder, paper and printing. This book provides an illustrated introduction to the many inventions to which China can lay claim, from the aforementioned seminal discoveries to mining technology, the production of silk and ceramics, and brilliant advances in astronomy, mathematics and medicine

Other Major Inventions (I). Iron and steel smelting ; Copper smelting and bronze ware ; Extraction and application of petroleum ; Discovery and mining of coal ; Pottery and porcelain ; Wine and liquor ; Sericulture ; Tea and tea culture

Other Major Inventions (II). Star catalogue and star atlas ; Observation of solar and lunar eclipses ; Surveying of the meridian ; Zhang Heng and his seismograph ; Guo Shoujing and his shoushi calendar ; The decimal and binary systems ; Zu Chongzhi and pi ; Equal temperament
